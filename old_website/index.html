Hi, I’m José.

Born in 1982, my journey into healing began as a teenager, driven by the weight of intense anxiety. I later realized I was a Highly Sensitive child—born with a deep empathy and intuition that I’ve spent the last two decades learning to navigate through therapy, somatic practices, and lived experience.

Since 2022, I have navigated the path of solo fatherhood. This journey of humility has been my greatest teacher in presence and nervous system regulation. Now, as I enter this next chapter, I am re-activating my roots as a Developer and Sound Engineer.

After a significant hiatus to focus on family, I am back at the keyboard and in the studio. I am currently "building in public," documenting my return to code and audio engineering as I merge these technical worlds with my somatic coaching practice.

"I am currently re-coding my life and my tools. I look at life through two lenses: the Somatic heart that feels the rhythm, and the Engineer’s mind that understands the system. I’m finding the signal in the noise, one line of code at a time."

Relationship & Somatic Coaching

My primary energy is dedicated to helping people navigate the complexities of human connection. I help individuals and couples "debug" the patterns that keep them stuck, using the body as our primary source of truth.

Relational Insight

We look at the System of your relationship. I help you identify the "phase cancellation" in your communication—the moments where you clash instead of harmonize—and help you rewrite those scripts for deeper connection.

In-Person Sessions (Safe Spaces)

I believe healing happens best where you feel most grounded. In-person sessions can be held in nature, a private home, or any quiet space that feels safe and supportive for you. Removing the clinical office environment allows the nervous system to open in its own time.

Remote & Async Coaching (Available to All)

Presence isn't limited by geography. I offer Virtual Meetings (Google Meet) and Asynchronous Voice Coaching for everyone—whether you are across the world or just prefer to process your growth from the comfort of your own space.
