About Me

Hi, I’m José. My path has always revolved around awareness, the body, and emotional clarity. I began meditating as a teenager, explored yoga in my 20s, and continued learning through therapy, somatic practices, and lived experience.

My work is not traditional therapy. It’s grounded, intuitive, somatic, and human. I help people slow down, feel safe in their bodies, and understand what’s truly happening inside them — especially in moments of emotional intensity, relationship struggles, and inner conflict.

At the core, I help people reconnect with a calmer, wiser part of themselves so they can move through life and relationships with more clarity and ease.

Sessions

My sessions blend somatic awareness, nervous system grounding, emotional insight, and simple meditation. Each session is practical, direct, and personalized.

  • 1:1 Insight Sessions: Explore what’s happening in your body, emotions, and nervous system. Together we uncover patterns beneath anxiety, fear, looping thoughts, or relational triggers — and reconnect you with clarity.
  • Relationship & Intimacy Clarity: Understand emotional patterns, attachment dynamics, conflict cycles, and the fears that show up in love. This is not couples therapy — it’s relational insight and somatic grounding.
  • Outdoor / Movement Sessions: Walking or being outdoors can help release tension and open deeper presence. Available upon request.
  • Remote Sessions: Meet from anywhere via Google Meet.

Fatherhood & Conscious Parenting

As a single father to a child with special needs, fatherhood has been one of my deepest paths of growth, humility, and awakening. I support fathers who want to show up with more presence, emotional awareness, and grounded leadership — without losing themselves in the process.

  • Fatherhood Coaching: Learn to stay regulated, present, and connected even in challenging moments.
  • Co‑parenting Support: Navigate communication, boundaries, and emotional balance with clarity and compassion.
  • Balancing Self & Family: Build a life where your purpose, wellbeing, and fatherhood strengthen each other instead of competing.

Studio & Recording

My home studio is a creative space where sound, breath, and voice support integration. Clients can record guided meditations, somatic practices, reflections, or grounding soundscapes. These recordings become tools to reconnect with your process at home.
