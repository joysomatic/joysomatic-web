About Me

Hi, I’m Jose. My journey began with meditation as a teenager and yoga in my 20s, and has been deepened by years of therapy, study, and personal exploration. I’ve walked the path of healing, intimacy, and growth myself — and now I hold space for others on their own journeys.

I see relationships as one of the highest paths for personal and spiritual growth. Through somatic practices, coaching, and sound, I help individuals and couples reconnect with themselves and with each other, cultivating compassion, awareness, and joy.

What I Offer

My coaching blends somatic awareness, Internal Family Systems, meditation, breath work, and the creative power of sound and music. Each session is tailored to your unique needs.

  • One-on-One Sessions: Discover tools to understand and integrate different parts of yourself with compassion.
  • Couples Coaching: Deepen intimacy, improve communication, and create a safe space for growth and sacred union.
  • Outdoors & Movement: Being outdoors and in motion can awaken deeper awareness, connection, and presence.
  • Remote Sessions: Work together via Google Meet from anywhere in the world.

Studio & Recording

My home studio is a space for sound exploration and healing. Here, clients can record guided meditations, soundscapes, or explore their own voice and music to support personal and relational growth. These recordings can be used as tools to witness your progress and continue your journey at home.
