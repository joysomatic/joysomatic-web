Hi, I’m José.

Born in 1982, my journey into healing began as a teenager, driven by the weight of intense anxiety. It took years of self-discovery to realize I was a Highly Sensitive child—born with a deep empathy and a sharp intuition that my environment wasn't yet equipped to guide. Today, those once-overwhelming "special gifts" are the very foundation of my work.

I explored yoga in my 20s and have spent my adult life dedicated to therapy, somatic practices, and deep lived experience. Since 2022, I have navigated the path of solo fatherhood—a journey of humility that has been my greatest teacher in presence, nervous system regulation, and grounded leadership.

During this same chapter, I returned to my roots as a Sound Engineer and Software Developer. I’ve discovered that whether I am mixing a track or writing code, I am using that same High Sensitivity to find the deep connections between human emotion and complex systems.

My work is not traditional therapy. It’s grounded, intuitive, somatic, and human. I help people slow down, feel safe in their bodies, and understand what’s truly happening inside them—especially those who feel "too much" or struggle with the intensity of inner conflict and relationship triggers.

"I look at life through two lenses: the Somatic heart that feels the rhythm, and the Engineer’s mind that understands the system. I help you find the signal in the noise."

At the core, I help you reconnect with a calmer, wiser part of yourself so you can move through your life and your relationships with more clarity, ease, and Joy."

Relationship & Somatic Coaching

My primary energy is dedicated to helping people navigate the complexities of human connection. I help individuals and couples "debug" the patterns that keep them stuck, using the body as our primary source of truth.

Relational Insight

We look at the System of your relationship. I help you identify the "phase cancellation" in your communication—the moments where you clash instead of harmonize—and help you rewrite those scripts for deeper connection.

In-Person Sessions (Safe Spaces)

I believe healing happens best where you feel most grounded. In-person sessions can be held in nature, a private home, or any quiet space that feels safe and supportive for you. Removing the clinical office environment allows the nervous system to open in its own time.

Remote & Async Coaching (Available to All)

Presence isn't limited by geography. I offer Virtual Meetings (Google Meet) and Asynchronous Voice Coaching for everyone—whether you are across the world or just prefer to process your growth from the comfort of your own space.

The JoySomatic Lab: Sound & Code

This is where technical precision meets somatic healing. I use my background in engineering to build tools and textures that support your growth.

  • Audio Integration: As a professional Sound Engineer, I create high-resolution grounding tracks and recorded reflections. Unlike generic meditations, these are mixed with an engineer’s ear to help your nervous system settle and integrate.
  • AI-Native Development: I am currently building the Synthesis Engine—a Python-based tool that helps people download, segment, and organize wisdom from podcasts. It’s about turning "information overload" into a manageable, personal library of insight.
  • Studio Recording: My home studio remains a space for local artists and creators to capture their voice with clarity and professional depth.

Solo Parenting & Conscious Leadership

Raising a son solo while navigating a career pivot is my daily practice. I offer a unique perspective for fathers who feel the weight of responsibility and want to lead their families with presence rather than pressure.
